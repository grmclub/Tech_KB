Symptoms of hyperglycaemia

    increased thirst and a dry mouth.
    needing to pee frequently.
    tiredness.
    blurred vision.
    unintentional weight loss.
    recurrent infections, such as thrush, bladder infections (cystitis) and skin infection

What are the big 3 symptoms of diabetes?
The three Ps are the most common signs of diabetes. Polyuria means frequent urination, polydipsia means excessive thirst, and polyphagia means excessive hunger

Over time, high blood sugar levels in type 2 diabetes can damage the eyes, kidneys, nerves and heart. This can happen because the pancreas doesn't make enough of a hormone called insulin that helps sugar enter the cells.
